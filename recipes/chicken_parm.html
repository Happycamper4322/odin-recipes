The Dish

Chicken Parmesan is delicous dish that is an American spin on the original Italian verison. Chicken Parmesan emerged in the early 20th century, mainly in Italian-American neighborhoods.

The orginal Italian recipe called for egg plant but Americans shifted to chicken because of its cheaper price and ease access. Please follow the recipe below to make a turly scrumptious dish deeply rooted in what is the epitome of the American experince.

Ingredients

  • Large skillet. I perfer cast iron.
  • Vegtable oil.
  • 2 jars of high quality red sauce.
  • 1 lb of fresh mozzarella
  • 1/2 lb of parmesan
  • 3 lbs of chicken breasts.
  • 1 cup of flour.
  • 1 cup of cornmeal.
  • 3 eggs.
  • what ever seasonings you desire. I use 1 Tbspn garlic salt, 1 Tbspn pepper, 2 tspn paprika, 1 tspn cayenne pepper, and 1 Tbspn Italian seasoning.

    Ultimatly season with your heart! This is just a guide.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Start boiling water for the pasta. Cook pasta per boxes instructions. Set aside in a deep pan when done.
  3. While water is boiling, flay the chicken breasts in half. pound them out so they are thin and even.
  4. In a bowl large enough to place a chicken breast in create the breading mixture. Combine the flour, cornmeal, garlic salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne pepper and Italian seasoning.
  5. In a small seperate bowl beat the three eggs.
  6. Now, put enought vegtable oil in your large skillet to creat a pool of oil atleast 3cm deep. Heat on medium high.

    You will know the oil is ready because it will start to shimmer and smoke a little.

  7. Once your oil is ready, coat a chicken breast in the egg mixture, then in the breading mixture, and then finally into the pan.
  8. Cook for about 4 mins then flip. Be sure to check internal temp with a thermometer. The temp shoudld be atleast 150F
  9. Repeat until all chicken is cooked.
  10. In the pan with the pasta, pour one jar of red sauce and stir it in. Place cooked chicken on top of the pasta.

    Top the chicken with mozzarella, parmesan, and the remaining jar of red sauce.

  11. Place pan in the oven for 10 mins or enough time for the cheese to get melty.
  12. Now the most critical step; ENJOY!
